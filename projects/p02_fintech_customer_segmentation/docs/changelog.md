## Key analytical findings

Initial fintech profiling identified:

- strong right-skew in financial exposure variables
- concentrated loan and savings distributions
- highly imbalanced risk tiers
- realistic mid-market income concentration
- operationally usable delinquency rate
- sparse high-risk customer population

Findings confirmed that:

- averages alone are insufficient
- customer groups are structurally heterogeneous
- segmentation analysis is analytically justified
